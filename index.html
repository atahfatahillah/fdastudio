About FDA Studio

FDA Studio was established in February 18, 2015 by two talented young people, namely Fatahillah as Founder and Abiyyu Yafi as Co-Founder. We have a motto "Still shine like the sun" philosophy of the motto that we have is a solution whenever and wherever you need us. When we established the FDA Studio in 2015, we are one of the students in one of the private vocational Telecommunications in West Jakarta. Our ambition is to create a job for the programmer-young and talented programmers in particular the vocational school students in Indonesia. Our vision for the future is petrified of small and medium entrepreneurs to build their businesses from offline business into an online business (Off to On) by providing services Web Design & Development. With the motto "Still shine like the sun" we believe will continue to grow from a startup industry into an industry that is able to compete in national and international arenas
